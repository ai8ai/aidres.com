Data Deletion Request

App: Nod: Daily Safety Check-In

Developer: omniDevX

Last updated: January 19, 2026

Overview

Nod: Daily Safety Check-In does not require users to create an account or log in. However, limited personal data is stored to provide the app’s safety check-in and emergency notification functionality.

This page explains how users can request deletion of their data.

Data Eligible for Deletion

Upon request, the following data associated with your device will be permanently deleted:

  • User-provided name
  • Device identifier used by the app
  • Emergency contact email address
  • All stored check-in timestamps

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please send an email with the subject line:

“Nod App – Data Deletion Request”

In your email, include:

  • The name you entered in the app (if any)
  • The emergency contact email associated with the app
  • Any additional information that may help identify your device

Send your request to:

Email: omniDevX@gmail.com

Deletion Timeline

Data deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receipt.

Data Retention

After deletion:

  • All personal data listed above is permanently removed from storage
  • No backup or archived copies are retained

Additional Information

Uninstalling the app will remove locally stored data from your device, but cloud-stored data must be deleted through the request process described above.
