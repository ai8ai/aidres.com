Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 19, 2026

Overview

Nod: Daily Safety Check-In is designed to help users, including seniors, confirm their daily well-being. The app collects and stores limited personal data solely to provide its safety check-in and emergency notification functionality.

Data Collected

The app may collect and store the following information:

  • User-provided name
  • Device identifier (used for identification without account registration)
  • Emergency contact email address
  • Check-in timestamps

How Data Is Used

  • To record daily check-ins initiated by the user
  • To determine whether a check-in has been missed for 48 hours
  • To send an email notification to the assigned emergency contact if a check-in is missed
  • To associate check-in data with a device without requiring login or registration

How Data Is Stored

  • Data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database service operated by Google.
  • The device identifier is used only to distinguish users and does not grant access to other device data.
  • No personal data is used for advertising, tracking, or analytics.

Automated Processing & Notifications

An automated background process runs periodically to check whether a user has missed a check-in for 48 hours.

  • If a missed check-in is detected, an email notification is sent to the designated emergency contact.
  • This process is executed using scheduled automation (GitHub Actions).

Email Delivery

Emergency notification emails are sent using Brevo, an external email delivery service.

  • The emergency contact email address is shared with Brevo solely for the purpose of sending notification emails.
  • Brevo processes email data according to its own privacy and security practices.

Data Sharing

Personal data is shared only as required to provide the app’s core functionality:

  • Google Firebase Firestore for secure data storage
  • Brevo for sending emergency notification emails

No personal data is sold or shared for marketing, advertising, or tracking purposes.

Internet Usage

Internet access is required to store check-in data and to send emergency notification emails. The app does not perform background analytics or unrelated data collection.

Permissions

The app does not request access to sensitive device permissions such as:

  • Camera
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Microphone

Data Retention & Deletion

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the app’s functionality. Users may delete all stored data by uninstalling the app or by contacting the developer.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children under the age of 18 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if new features or services are introduced. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
